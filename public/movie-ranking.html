The Best Movies According to Sumit

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Christmas Carol

A wonderful movie adapation of the classic tale. I was really surprised to find out Jim Carrey had voiced the main character.

Your Name

A beautifully animated movie with an interesting story, fun characters and a delightful soundtrack

Spider-Man: No Way Home

All your favourite live-action Spider-Men in one movie. One of the best Spider-Man and comic book movies!
